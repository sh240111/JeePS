For Filipinos, jeepneys have long been part of everyday lives. They’re affordable, efficient, and one of the most accessible ways to move around the city. Though, for many commuters—especially on unfamiliar routes—figuring out where to ride and where to get off can be confusing.

But worry not, because JeePS: Routa de mga Vivientes is here to make commuting easier in Zamboanga City. We help Zamboangueño commuters navigate jeepney routes by showing clear directions to and from paradahans (jeepney parking lots) across the city.

Our mission is straightforward: to assist you in reaching your destination with reduced stress, minimized uncertainty, and less time lost. Whether you’re exploring a different path or simply seeking a more comfortable journey, JeePS is here to guide you in every step of the way.
